Who We Are

A Boutique Consultancy Built on 18 Years of Practice

Esquire Consultants is an established Training, HR and Management Consultancy provider based in Nairobi, Kenya. We solve real organizational challenges through cutting-edge, innovative consultancy that provokes a new way of doing things.

Started in 2008 in Thika town, the firm converted to a Limited Company in 2014 to expand its scope across public sector, NGO and corporate clientele — growing our service menu into Talent Search & Placement, HR Advisory, Surveys & Research, Career Advisory and Governance & Leadership Consultancy.
